================================================================================
CLARIFIED STOREFRONT / PAYMENT MODEL
================================================================================

- Each operator runs their own service storefront inside NeighborDogs.
- The operator is the shop manager.
- Operators manage walk/service prices in NeighborDogs Admin (operator_prices).
- Customers see operator current prices on the public booking page (/book/[slug]).
- Checkout uses current operator price when customer submits the order (server-side
  re-read at checkout session create).
- Walk payments are separate from the $149/month operator subscription.
- Walk payments use Stripe Connect Standard accounts and direct charges.
- Operator is merchant of record; operator pays Stripe card processing fees; operator
  Stripe balance bears refunds and disputes.
- Stripe processes the customer credit card on the operator connected account.
- Operators connect/onboard directly with Stripe for payout/banking information.
- NeighborDogs does not collect or store operator banking information.
- Bryan / NeighborDogs receives $0 from walk payments — no walk fee, percentage,
  commission, platform fee, application fee, or cut.
- Walk checkout must not set application_fee_amount, transfer_data.destination,
  or on_behalf_of. NeighborDogs is the software layer only.
- Walk prices are NOT managed via Stripe Dashboard Products/Prices (code uses inline
  price_data).

LOCKED SAAS:
- Operators pay NeighborDogs $149/month for software access (subscription checkout).
- Operators absorb Stripe card processing fees.
- STRIPE_WALK_PLATFORM_FEE_PERCENT is not part of this release.

FORBIDDEN COPY:
- Do not say payment happens outside the app.
- Do not say payment is handled directly with the walker.
- Do not say NeighborDogs takes a walk fee/cut/commission/platform/application fee.
