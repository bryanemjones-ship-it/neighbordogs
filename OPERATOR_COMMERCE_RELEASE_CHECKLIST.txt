================================================================================
NEIGHBORDOGS PAYMENT MODEL — FINAL FOR THIS RELEASE
================================================================================

- Operators pay NeighborDogs $149/month for software access.
- Clients pay operators for walks through the app.
- Operators receive walk payments through Stripe Connect.
- Operators absorb Stripe card processing fees.
- NeighborDogs receives $0 from walk payments.
- NeighborDogs does not receive any walk percentage, commission, platform fee,
  application fee, or cut.
- Stripe subscription checkout is used for the $149/month operator subscription.
- Stripe Connect is used only so operators can receive client walk payments
  through the app.
- Walk checkout must not set application_fee_amount.
- STRIPE_WALK_PLATFORM_FEE_PERCENT is not part of the current release model.

Copy gates (must NOT appear in production booking/payment UX or docs):
- Do not say payment happens outside the app.
- Do not say payment is handled directly with the walker.
- Do not say NeighborDogs takes a walk fee, cut, commission, platform fee, or
  application fee.
