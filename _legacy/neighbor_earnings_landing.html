What year 5 looks like

Territory exit value

You built an asset.

When you are ready to move on, your territory lists on the AppStack marketplace. The customer database transfers with the sale. You keep 90% of the sale price.

Valuation is based on of annual SDE — Seller's Discretionary Earnings. Small service businesses with recurring clients and transferable customer databases command 2–3×. Solo physical service businesses without systematization: 1.5–2×. The Neighbor platform sits in the middle — scheduling, payments, and reporting are systematized, making the business genuinely transferable.⁴
