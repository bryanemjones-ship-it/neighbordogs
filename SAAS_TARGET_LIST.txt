Scheduling + Notification Platform

===============================================================================
HOW TO READ THIS LIST
===============================================================================

Priority Score (1-5 stars) based on:
  ★ = # of locations (more = bigger deal)
  ★ = Tech sophistication (less = more likely to need you)
  ★ = Recurring scheduling model (vs one-off)
  ★ = Fit with current platform features
  ★ = Accessibility (can you actually reach decision-makers?)

Revenue estimate = locations × $49-99/mo per location

SALES APPROACH NOTES
===============================================================================

THE PITCH (customize per vertical):
"We built a scheduling and notification platform that reduces no-shows,
automates customer communication, and generates 5-star reviews — all
branded to your business. Your [stylists/techs/tutors/cleaners] get
a simple mobile dashboard. Your customers get branded text confirmations,
day-before reminders, 'on the way' alerts, and automatic review requests
after every appointment. $49-99/month per location. One prevented
no-show pays for the entire year."

WHO TO CONTACT:
- Franchise Development VP (cares about franchisee satisfaction)
- Chief Technology Officer / VP Operations (cares about efficiency)
- Franchisee Advisory Council members (influencers who talk to corporate)
- Individual multi-unit franchisees (own 5-20 locations, can adopt
  independently even without corporate mandate)

PROOF POINTS TO BUILD:
- Before/after no-show rates
- Review generation numbers (X reviews generated per month)
- Customer satisfaction scores
- Time saved per location per week on manual communication

ENTRY STRATEGY:
1. Start with 2-3 individual franchisees (any brand) as beta customers
2. Build case studies with real numbers
3. Approach franchise corporate with proven ROI data
4. Negotiate enterprise deal for full brand rollout
5. Use first brand win to approach parent company for multi-brand deal
