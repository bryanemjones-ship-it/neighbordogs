Policies

Service Policies

Reservations: Must be made by midnight prior to service (except Emergency Walk).

Service Area: 1.5-mile radius of Six Forks Rd & Lassiter Mill Rd, Raleigh NC.

New Customers: Must complete an in-person Meet & Greet (10–15 min) before any walks.

Payment: Via Venmo. Weekly packages must be paid by Sunday midnight.

Emergency Walks: Existing customers only. 5:00–6:30 PM. $50/dog.

Multi-Dog Households: First dog at full price. Each additional dog just +$10 per walk (max 3 dogs).

Safety: Service may be refused or ended for aggressive dog behavior.

Insurance: We are fully insured and bonded for your peace of mind. Your home and your pup are protected every single visit.
